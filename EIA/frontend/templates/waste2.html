Recycling Guidance

Why Recycling is Important

Recycling helps conserve natural resources, reduces waste, and lowers pollution. It's an essential step towards a sustainable future.

How to Recycle Properly

  • Separate paper, plastic, and metal materials.
  • Make sure containers are clean and dry.
  • Check local recycling rules for specific guidelines.

Where to Recycle

Find your nearest recycling center online or check with your local municipality for collection schedules.
